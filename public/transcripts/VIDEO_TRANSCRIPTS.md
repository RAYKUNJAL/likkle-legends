## How to Create Missing Transcripts

### For YouTube Videos

1. **Check for Auto-captions:**
   - Open the YouTube video
   - Click the CC button (Closed Captions)
   - If auto-captions exist, download or transcribe them

2. **Manual Transcription:**
   - Watch the video with headphones
   - Pause frequently to transcribe accurately
   - Note timestamps for each segment
   - Include sound descriptions in [brackets]

3. **Professional Services:**
   - **Rev.com**: Upload video, get transcripts in 24 hours
   - **Descript**: Upload video, AI transcribes automatically
   - **3PlayMedia**: Professional caption and transcript service
   - **YouTube's Official Captions**: May be available via YouTube Studio

### For Self-Hosted Videos

1. Use transcription service (Rev, Descript, Google Cloud Speech-to-Text)
2. Create .vtt file with timestamps
3. Test captions sync with video playback
4. Place files in `/public/captions/` directory

## Web Accessibility Standards

This project complies with:

- **WCAG 2.1 Level AA**: Video captions required for all video content
- **ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act)**: Captions required for public video content
- **COPPA**: Child-friendly, safe content standards
- **UK Equality Act**: Equal access to multimedia content
